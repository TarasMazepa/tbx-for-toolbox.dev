Shortcuts & Behavior

Infinity Note has a unique top-down entry flow.

  • Enter Insert line at top of current block
  • Shift + Enter Regular new line
  • Shift + Alt + ↑ Move line(s) up
  • Shift + Alt + ↓ Move line(s) down
  • Cmd/Ctrl + Backspace Delete selected line(s)
Press Enter to insert a new line at the top of the current block. Shift+Enter for regular new line.
